The CRDs carry the `helm.sh/resource-policy: keep` annotation, so
`helm uninstall` will leave them in place. Any existing Valkey or
SentinelQuorum custom resources continue to exist after uninstall —
the operator just stops reconciling them.

To remove the CRDs explicitly (this cascade-deletes every Valkey and
SentinelQuorum CR in the cluster):

  kubectl get valkeys -A
  kubectl get sentinelquorums -A
  kubectl delete crd valkeys.velkir.ioxie.dev sentinelquorums.velkir.ioxie.dev

To opt out of the keep behaviour for future installs:

  --set crds.keep=false
